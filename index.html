Education & cultural center providing free resources & opportunities to general public, esp. to youth. It also organizes events and programs based on English, entrepreneurship and women empowerment.

Sustainable Education Foundation

The Sustainable Education Foundation is launched with the aim of building a connector platform to bring Sri Lankan subject matter experts in academia and industry to build a more sustainable education ecosystem in Sri Lanka

STEM Up Educational Foundation

The STEMUP Foundation is a not for profit organization whose mission is to inspire and engage students to become interested in pursuing STEM degrees and careers. Their mission is fulfilled through the programs and resources we offer to the South Asian Community.

Lanka Software Foundation

Lanka Software Foundation is a non-profit company which focuses on providing a platform for Sri Lanka Software engineers to create open source technology that would impact the world of software
